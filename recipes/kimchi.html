Kimchi

The main source of information for this recipe is allrecipes.com

Description

Kimchi is a lacto-fermented dish from the Korean tradition. It is usually composed of a napa cabbage and Korean radish as a base, with additions like ginger, garlic, and spring oninons.

I love this kind of food. Here I will be going through a recipe I found--the source is just under the first photo--and finally learning how to make it myself. The winter is not far, and I want to stock up on pickles and such delicious fermented foods.
